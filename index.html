Top 5 strangest Pokemons

Trubbish

A literal bag of trash brought to life. Trubbish is born from garbage and industrial waste, releasing a foul stench when threatened. Despite its off-putting nature, it somehow has fans who find it... oddly adorable.

Mr. Mime

With its humanoid shape, painted face, and invisible walls, Mr. Mime sits deep in the uncanny valley. Its movements mimic those of a classic mime, and its psychic abilities are as unsettling as its toothy smile

Bruxish

A neon-colored fish with dazzling scales and a disturbing grin full of sharp teeth. Bruxish uses psychic waves to stun prey, but its clashing color palette and aggressive look make it one of the most bizarre aquatic Pokémon.

Stunfisk (Galarian Form)

Looking like a rusty bear trap with eyes, Galarian Stunfisk hides on the ground and clamps onto anything that steps on it. It’s part Ground/Steel and has a troll-like nature that fits its odd appearance.

Porygon-Z

A digital Pokémon that glitched out. Designed as a software upgrade to the original Porygon, something went wrong, and Porygon-Z now behaves unpredictably, with jerky movements and an eerie, hollow stare.
